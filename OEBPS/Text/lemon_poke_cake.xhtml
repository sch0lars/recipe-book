Lemon Poke Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages lemon flavored Jell-O mix
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions

  1. Bake cake as directed. When done, use a meat fork to poke holes across the top of the entire cake. Allow to cool.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together 1 box gelatin, 1 cup hot water, and 1 cup cold water. Pour over top of cake. Refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours.
  3. Mix remaining box of gelatin, pudding mix, milk, and vanilla together. Beat well. Fold whipped topping into this mixture, and spread on top of cake. Chill in refrigerator until serving.

Note: This can also be made with orange cake mix and orange Jell-O. If you cannot find an orange cake mix, use a package of lemon cake mix and add 1 small envelope of orange drink mix powder.
