Stewed Squash

Ingredients

  • Stick of butter
  • 1- 2 onions
  • 8 Squash

Directions

  1. Slice or Chop Onion
  2. Slice Squash
  3. Cut up a stick of butter into smaller pieces and put into pot.
  4. Turn on stove to low-medium heat
  5. When butter starts to melt, add onions
  6. Stir and cook on low-medium heat for 2-3 minutes
  7. Add sliced squash and mix onion and butter with squash
  8. Place lid on pot. Continue to stir over the next 20-25 minutes
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
