Instant Pot Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 steaks
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. First, combine all seasonings in a small bowl.
  2. Pat steaks dry with paper towels. Rub seasonings all over both sides of steaks.
  3. Press saute on Instant Pot. Add olive oil to pot.
  4. Once oil is hot, place steaks in pot. Sear for 1 to 2 minutes on each side.
  5. Press cancel. Use tongs to remove steak from pot onto plate.
  6. Pour 1 cup water down into pot and scrape bottom of pot with wooden spoon to deglaze it.
  7. Place trivet inside pot. Place steaks on top of trivet.
  8. Secure lid and turn vent to sealing. Pressure cook on high for 3 minutes for medium rare, up to 12 minutes for well done.
  9. When timer goes off, carefully turn valve to venting and manually release pressure.
  10. Finally, carefully remove steaks from pot. Allow steaks to rest for 5 minutes before slicing into to lock in those juices.
