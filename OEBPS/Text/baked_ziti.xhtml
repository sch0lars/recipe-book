Baked Ziti

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) package provolone cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 3/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 (6 ounce) package mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Prepare spaghetti sauce according to recipe.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. In the prepared dish, layer 1/2 of the cooked pasta, provolone cheese, sour cream, cottage cheese, and a little less than 1/2 of the spaghetti sauce. Then layer the rest of the pasta, mozzarella cheese, remaining meat mixture, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes, or until heated through and cheeses are melted.
