Baked Chicken Chimichangas

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 8 ounces pepper jack cheese, shredded
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 pound cooked chicken, shredded
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9 x 13 dish with cooking spray.
  2. Stir together the cream cheese, Pepper jack cheese and taco seasoning. Fold in the chicken. Divide among the flour tortillas. Fold the sides in and roll up each tortilla.
  3. Lay the chimichanga seam side down in the dish. Spray the tops of the tortilla with cooking spray.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Turn the chimichanga over and bake an additional 15 minutes.
  5. Serve with shredded cheese, sliced green onions, sour cream and salsa.
