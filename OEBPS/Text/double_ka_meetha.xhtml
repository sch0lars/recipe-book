Double Ka Meetha (Indian Bread Pudding)

Ingredients

  • 5 Bread slices, sliced into triangles with crusts removed
  • 4 tablespoon Oil for frying
  • 12 oz Evaporated milk
  • 7 oz Condensed milk
  • 1 tsp Cardamom
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 2 tablespoon Mixed nuts, chopped (optional)

Directions

  1. In a sauce pan, combine the evaporated milk and condensed milk. Mix it well and bring it to a gentle boil. Keep cooking until is creamy.
  2. Add the cardamom and continue to cook until it reaches a thick and creamy consistency.
  3. Let the mixture cool down to room temperature.
  4. Meanwhile, combine the sugar and the water in a pan and bring it to a boil. Continue to boil until the syrup is sticky.
  5. Pan fry the bread and nuts in oil.
  6. Dip the fried bread in the sugar syrup and then drizzle the milk mixture over each bread piece. Top with chopped nuts and serve.
