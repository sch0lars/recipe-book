Peas and Carrots

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 lb. carrots, peeled and thinly sliced on the bias
  • 3/4 tsp.kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp.freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 c. water
  • 1 Tbsp. honey
  • 2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard, preferably coarse-ground
  • 10 oz. peas
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • Freshly grated Pecorino or Parmesan, for serving (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add carrots, salt, and pepper and stir until well-coated in butter, 2 miinutes. Add water and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid is reduced by half, 4 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add honey and mustard and stir until evenly combined, then add peas. Cook, stirring frequently, until peas are warmed through and sauce clings to veggies, about 5 minutes more. Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in lemon juice and top with cheese, if desired, before serving.
