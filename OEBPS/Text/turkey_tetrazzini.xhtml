Turkey Tetrazzini

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, cubed
  • 1 can (10-1/2 ounces) condensed chicken broth, undiluted
  • 2 cups chopped cooked turkey or chicken
  • 1 jar (2 ounces) diced pimientos, drained
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions; drain. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat butter over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook 6-8 minutes or until onion is tender.
  2. Add cream cheese and broth; cook, uncovered, over low heat 4-6 minutes or until blended, stirring occasionally. Add turkey, pimientos, salt and spaghetti; heat through, tossing to coat. Add cheedar cheese and stir until melted.
