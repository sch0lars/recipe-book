Cajun Pasta

Ingredients

  • 16 oz rotini pasta (or any short pasta of choice)
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 13 oz Andouille sausage
  • 4 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for extra spice)
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)

Directions

  1. Season shrimp and sausage with 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the sausage until and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt 2 tbsp butter and cook the shrimp until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low, then add minced garlic to the skillet. Saute for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Stir in heavy cream, milk, remaining butter, Parmesan cheese, cheddar cheese, smoked paprika, and the remaining Cajun seasoning. Mix well until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Add the cooked pasta, chicken, and shrimp to the skillet. Toss to coat evenly with the creamy Cajun sauce.
  7. Adjust seasoning with salt and red pepper flakes, if desired.
