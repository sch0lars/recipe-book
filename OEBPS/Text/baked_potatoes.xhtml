Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 - 2 russet potatoes
  • 1 cup (250ml) cold water

Directions

  1. Scrub the potatoes thoroughly under cold running water, and poke deep holes all over the potatoes with a fork.
  2. Add 1 cup (250ml) of cold water, and a trivet in Instant Pot. Then, place the potatoes on the trivet. Close lid, then turn Venting Knob to Sealing position. Pressure cook on high for 30 minutes. Manually release pressure as soon as the Instant Pot beeps.
