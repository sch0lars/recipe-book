Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 large Russet potatoes, cubed
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large pot, bring lightly salted water to a boil. Add cubed potatoes and continue boiling until potatoes are soft.
  2. Drain potoatoes and transfer to a large mixing bowl. Lightly mash potatoes.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and add minced garlic. Let the garlic cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes, and pour in heavy cream. Add salt and pepper. Stir heavy cream until sauce thickens and the garlic is soft. Remove from heat.
  4. Pour the garlic sauce mixture over the potatoes and continue mashing until the sauce has been thoroughly mixed into the potoatoes. Serve warm.
