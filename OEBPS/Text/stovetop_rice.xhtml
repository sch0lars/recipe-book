Stovetop Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup water
  • 1 cup rice

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, rinse rice thoroughly with water, filling the bowl up and stirring the rice around before draining the water. Repeat several times until rice water pours out clean.
  2. In a saucepan, bring 1 3/4 cup of water to a boil.
  3. Add rice and cover saucepan. Simmer rice undisturbed for 18 minutes.
  4. Turn off heat, remove lid, and fluff the rice.
  5. Cover saucepan again and let rice sit another 5 minutes before serving.
