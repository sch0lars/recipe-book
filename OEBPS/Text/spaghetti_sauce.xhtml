Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground mild Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp rosemary
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar or to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium-high heat, add beef, sausage, onion, basil, and rosemary and cook until mostly browned, about 5 minutes. Drain if desired. Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, sugar, and bay leaf. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
