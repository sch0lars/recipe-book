Rotini Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 16 oz rotini pasta, (1 box)
  • 4 oz cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 4 oz mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 8 oz pepperoni slices, quartered
  • 1 cup Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta per package directions. Drain pasta and run under cold water, then place pasta in a large bowl.
  2. While the pasta is cooking prep the remaining ingredients. When it is done, mix in the cheese, peppers, tomatoes, onion, pepperoni, and basil.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.
  4. Serve immediately or chill, then toss before serving.
