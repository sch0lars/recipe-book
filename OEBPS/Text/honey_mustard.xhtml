Honey Mustard Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, mustard, honey, and lemon juice together in a small bowl.
  3. Store covered in the refrigerator. Serve as a dip or salad dressing.
